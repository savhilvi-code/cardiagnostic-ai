About PULS CarDiagnostic AI

PULS is an AI-powered vehicle diagnostic assistant designed to help drivers and mechanics identify automotive problems faster. The platform combines AI analysis, DTC code interpretation, repair manuals, service information, and real-world repair cases to provide practical diagnostic recommendations.

What PULS helps with

Use PULS to understand symptoms, review error codes, and navigate repair knowledge with a practical, car-focused assistant.
